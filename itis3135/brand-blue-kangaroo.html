Blue Kangaroo Enterprises is a industry leader in software engineering, and interactive webpage design. We create business solutions for companies and businesses that do not have the resources to hire large teams of developers. Our goal is to provide our customers with excellent service, and instantaneous technical support for their business needs. Blue Kangaroo Enterprises also seeks to invest and support environmentally conscious businesses who focus on reducing the impacts of climate change.

Our Achievements:

  • Our company has created and developed personalized interactive websites for over 1000 businesses and individuals!
  • In our effort to support action against climate change, our company has donated more than 5 million dollars to charitable organizations who help clean our environment.
  • Our company employs over 1,500 people from all over the world. We have offices in over a dozen countries that allow us to meet our customers needs no matter their location or timezone.
  • Our company works closely with NASA to create free resources for science and STEM educators of all kinds.
  • In 2020, Blue Kangaroo Enterprises was named the number one most environmentally friendly company by the Nature Conservancy magazine.

Blue Kangaroo Enterprises is always looking for new environmentally conscious clients to help develop software, webpages, or other technical projects. Our focus is to help our customers through the tedious development process, so that they can focus on the important work they are doing.
